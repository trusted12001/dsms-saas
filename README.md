# DSMS – Driving School Management System

DSMS is a multi-tenant SaaS platform built with Laravel 12, designed to help driving schools manage their daily operations in real time.

---

## 🚀 Overview

DSMS enables driving schools to manage:

- students
- instructors
- lesson scheduling
- payments and transactions
- sales and accounts
- reports and business performance

Each driving school operates as an independent tenant within the platform.

---

## 🧱 Architecture

- Laravel 12
- Blade templates
- MySQL
- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture

### Workspaces

- **Super Admin** → platform owner
- **Tenant Workspace** → individual driving schools

### Tenant Roles

- Business Owner
- Manager
- Sales
- Accounts

---

## 🎯 Project Goal

To provide a real-time operational and financial management system for driving schools, ensuring full visibility into business activities as they happen.
